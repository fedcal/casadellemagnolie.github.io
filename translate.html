Description

The apartment is located in Baia Verde, one of the most famous coastal stretches of Gallipoli (LE), just 150 m from a wide beach of fine sand, where bathing establishments alternate, equipped with all comforts, at times of free beach. The large and bright apartment is located in Viale dei Tigli 56, on the second floor of an elegant, recently renovated building, equipped with a lift and with free parking in the square in front. On a useful area of ​​103 square meters, the apartment with 8 beds, consists of a kitchen with a balcony, a large living room with a TV, a sofa bed, as well as a bunk bed and a second balcony; two bathrooms, both with shower, two large bedrooms, each equipped with a television, a double bed and a single bed. Both the living area and the sleeping area have air conditioning and each room has a large window with mosquito net. The furniture is comfortable and refined. The kitchen has crockery for 8 people, refrigerator with small freezer, electric oven, 4 burner stove, TV and dining table. Hair dryers are available in the bathrooms and a washing machine. Sheets and towels will be provided upon request. Iron and ironing board is also provided.
